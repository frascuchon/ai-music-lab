A classical piece that could serve as a soundtrack, this composition
is characterized by its cinematic quality, evoking a sense of
adventure, drama, and epic action. With a string ensemble of viola,
violin, and cello at the forefront, it unfolds at an Andante pace in
4/4 time, set in the key of C major. The chord progression of Dm, D,
G, C, and F lends to its emotive narrative.
